About Us

Welcome to our House Price and Rent Prediction Website !

We are a team of final year students who have developed this website as part of our project. Our website aims to provide accurate predictions for house prices and rents, helping users make informed decisions when buying, selling, or renting properties. We have utilized our knowledge and expertise in these areas to develop a robust and reliable prediction model that takes into consideration various factors affecting house prices and rents, such as location, property size, etc.

We have taken great care to ensure the accuracy and reliability of our predictions by using state-of-the-art algorithms. However, it's important to note that our predictions are based on historical data, and actual prices or rents may vary due to various factors, such as changes in the local real estate market, economic conditions, or other unforeseen circumstances.
